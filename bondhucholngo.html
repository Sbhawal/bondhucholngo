About Us

"A few people of integrity can go a long way."

This line, obviously grabs attention but also speaks off a lot about our purpose and our initiative.. "BONDHU CHOL" - the brain child of Arijit Roy ,a young creative mind, and an aspiring painter, started off from a mere whatsapp group. A group of six friends (Arijit, Devsmithaa, Nirmalya, Rohan, Samadrito, Sayan) with a vision of doing something new, formed the core of this initiative.

In "Bondhu chol" we friends aim towards the greater good by raising funds and helping the unpriviledged and making their lives a little easier.

We are still a young organization and you can help us grow by showing your much appreciated support in our cause. Every penny counts.
